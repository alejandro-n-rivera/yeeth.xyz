  • Development Sept. 2021 - Dec. 2021
    What is Yeeth? Yeeth is an automated, all-in-one blockchain pen-testing and security assessment framework capable of performing static and dynamic analysis. Our product will implement refined tools that will work in parallel to determine the healthiness of a blockchain project and alert on any potential vulnerabilities a security researcher may want to look into. The framework makes use of symbolic execution and can easily fit within the workflow of a developer’s project so it is out of the way and in the background, only notifying if there are any vulnerabilities to be detected.
